About Me

I’m Gergely Visnyei — a DJ and record producer currently based in Amsterdam. My passion for electronic music began at an early age, and it quickly evolved into a lifelong creative journey.

My first breakthrough came when I participated in a local DJ competition in Enschede, which opened the doors to a professional career. Since then, I’ve released several EPs and studio albums that blend melodic house, techno, and organic sounds — always pushing the boundaries of creativity.

I’m also the co-owner of two Amsterdam-based record labels, Rejected and Green, where I continue to collaborate with incredible artists and producers.

Music for me is not just rhythm and sound — it’s storytelling. Each track I create is a reflection of emotion, energy, and evolution.
